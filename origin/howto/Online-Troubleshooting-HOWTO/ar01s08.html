8. Online User's Manuals

Some of the major Linux distributions, Window Managers and Desktop Environments publish their user's manual online. If the entire manual is not online, installation and configuration guides are typically found at the relevant website. This also applies to major applications like the Gimp (GNU Image Manipulation Program) or Apache.

So, for those users who do not have the printed manual, a wealth of installation and configuration guidance is generally available online.
