14. Samples

This section gives some simple SGML examples you could copy. Read the source to see how it was done.

14.1 Lists

Lists appears many times, in a number of formats:

Unlisted bullets:

  • Apples
  • Oranges
  • Bananas

Tagged lists

Fruits

such as apples, oranges, and more.

Nuts

Don't eat too many; you are what you eat.

Vegetables

Potatos are spelled with care.
